About OCYM Kollam

Orthodox Christian Youth Movement (OCYM) Kollam is a vibrant youth organization working under the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. OCYM aims to nurture spiritual growth, leadership, fellowship, and social responsibility among young people.

Through prayer meetings, leadership camps, cultural programs, charity initiatives, and youth gatherings, OCYM Kollam empowers the youth to live a Christ-centered life while serving society with love and compassion.
