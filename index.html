The Orthodox Christian Youth Movement (OCYM) is the premier youth organization of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church, empowering young believers to serve Christ and society.

Our Mission

To lead young people to Christ, encouraging active involvement in the Orthodox Church and building morally upright Christian leaders.

🎯

Our Vision

A vibrant, spirit-filled youth community that bears witness to Christian values through social action, charity, and fellowship.
