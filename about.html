About OCYM Kollam

Orthodox Christian Youth Movement (OCYM) Kollam is a dynamic youth organization functioning under the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. It aims to nurture young minds with spiritual discipline, leadership qualities, and strong moral values.

OCYM Kollam actively engages in prayer meetings, leadership camps, cultural programs, social service activities, charity initiatives, and youth gatherings that strengthen unity and fellowship among young members.

Through faith, service, and commitment, OCYM Kollam strives to build responsible citizens who live according to Christian values while contributing positively to society.
