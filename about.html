Our Heritage & Mission

About OCYM Kollam Diocese

The Orthodox Christian Youth Movement (OCYM) of Kollam Diocese functions as the youth wing of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church.

✝

Spiritual Fellowship & Leadership

OCYM Kollam actively engages in prayer meetings, leadership camps, cultural programs, social service activities, charity initiatives, and youth gatherings that strengthen unity and fellowship among young members across 64 parishes.

📖

Spiritual Formation

Nurturing biblical study, Orthodox liturgy awareness, prayer routines, and spiritual discipline among young adults.

🌱

Leadership Development

Equipping youth with organizational skills, public speaking, decision-making, and responsible parish governance.

❤️

Social Outreach & Charity

Engaging in blood donation drives, educational scholarships, medical aid, and disaster relief operations.
